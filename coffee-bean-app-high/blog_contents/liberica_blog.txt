<p style='color: #000000; font-family: Poppins, Arial; font-size: 18px; text-align: justify;'>Grown primarily in the Philippines and Malaysia, Liberica thrives in tropical climates and is known for its bold and distinctive taste. The flavor is often described as being more woody and earthy, with an exciting complexity that can keep you coming back for more.</p>
